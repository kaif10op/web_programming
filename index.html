What is Trading?

Trading is the process of buying and selling financial instruments like stocks, commodities, currencies, or cryptocurrencies. It involves analyzing the market, predicting price movements, and making decisions to earn profits. Trading can be done by individuals or institutions and requires a good understanding of the market dynamics.

Types of Trading

There are several types of trading styles based on strategy and time horizon:

1. Day Trading: Buying and selling within the same day.
2. Swing Trading: Holding trades for several days or weeks.
3. Position Trading: Long-term trading based on fundamentals.
4. Scalping: Making quick trades to capture small price movements.
5. Algorithmic Trading: Automated trading using pre-programmed strategies.
